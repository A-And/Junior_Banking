Lloyds Junior Banking is a way for parents to have control over their children's finances.

It's a fun way to teach your children the value of saving.

Two types of interfaces allow seamless use. Just log in and manage your finances!

Money management system via two types of accounts. Spend money in your 'Pocket' and when you want to save money in your 'Stash' will be automatically applied to your Goals!

Our Goals and Awards system brings the 'fun' to the 'fundamentals of finance'. By setting savings Goals your child will discover the benefits of saving money.

Select Safe Neighbourhood ATMs where you would like your children to withdraw money.

Get banking advice and tips Our helpful Banking Guide will be a gentle introduction to the world of banking.

Start learning with Lloyds Junior Banking.
